About me

Hey, I’m Sem.

I’m a game designer more focused on the technical side of things.

I’ve always been a curious kid, and I’ve always loved gaming, and now I’m lucky enough to be able to combine the two of those into a field that I love.

I love prototyping and experimenting with all kinds of tricks and tools, making me a jack of all trades and master of some.

My passion lies in both the mechanical and the human brain, and the conversation between them. I love picking both of these brains and coming up with interesting solutions to the problems they cause.

Outside of gaming I enjoy watching F1, putting new outfits together even though I’m colorblind, and learning more about topics like neurology and neural networks.

Feel free to reach out to me about whatever! I’m always interested in meeting new people!
